# EigenSecret Product Manual v1.0



## Register

1.Use Gmail to log in
<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/Use%20Gmail%20to%20log%20in.png" width="270px"></div>

2.Select login mailbox
<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/Choose%20Google%20Account.png" width="270px"></div>

3.Perform key backup according to the message at the top
<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/backup%20according%20to%20the%20message%20at%20the%20top.png" width="270px"></div>

4.Select key backup content: you can select secret recovery phrase or private key, and set the name and description of the backup. As follows, make a backup by secret recovery phrase.

<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/Select%20key%20backup%20content.png" width="270px"></div>

5.2FA Attachment: Download Google Authenticator through App Store. Scan the QR code in the Google Authenticator page, then enter 6-digit code corresponding to EigenNetwork in Google Authenticator.

<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/2FA%20Attachment.png" width="270px"></div>

6.If you jump to the following page, it means that you have completed the attachment through Google Authenticator and the verification is successful. Click confirm to proceed with the key backup process.

<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/Authenticator%20verified%20successfully.png" width="270px"></div>

<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/Social%20Recovery.png" width="270px"></div>

7.Add friends and wait for the their reply to confirm the relationship. Click refresh to see the status of friends.

<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/Add%20friends.png" width="270px"></div>

8. After sending the secret share to a friend by email, the friend logs in to https://secret.ieigen.com/ and clicks on the avatar icon in the upper right corner-Friends.

<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/Friend's%20consent%20operation.png" width="270px"></div>

9.Select at least two friends, send the secret share to the their mailbox, and authorize Gmail.

<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/Choose%20Friends.png" width="270px"></div>

<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/Set%20threshold.png" width="270px"></div>

10.Google Authorization

<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/Authorization.png" width="270px"></div>

11.Complete authorization on Google Page

<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/Google%20hasn%E2%80%99t%20verified%20this%20app.png" width="270px"></div>

12.Tick authorized access content. Just select all by default.

<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/Select%20what%20ieigen%20can%20access.png" width="270px"></div>

13.Click send email

<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/Authorization.png" width="270px"></div>

14.The secret share is sent successfully. The selected friends will receive secret share. You can recover the key through the social recovery.

<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/The%20secret%20share%20is%20sent%20successfully.png" width="270px"></div>


## Recovery secrets

1.Click Social Recovery

<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/Click%20Social%20Recovery.png" width="270px"></div>

2.Select the backup to recover

<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/Select%20the%20backup%20to%20recover.png" width="270px"></div>

3.Enter 6-digit code as Google Authenticator code

<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/Enter%206-digit%20code%20as%20Google%20Authenticator%20code.png" width="270px"></div>

4.Click the restore key, and input the corresponding secret share that has beensent to the friend's mailbox previously. Then click confirm to recover the secret recovery phrase or private key

<div align=left><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ieigen/secret/master/docs/images/Input%20the%20restore%20key.png" width="270px"></div>

5. Double click the key to clip it and paste it into your wallets
